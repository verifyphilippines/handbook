# About Vouch Philippines 🇵🇭

!['A view from the top of Pescador Island (Unsplash)' by Ken Suarez is made available under the Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication."](https://mirror.tangled.network/xrpc/sh.tangled.git.temp.getBlob?path=images/A_view_from_the_top_of_Pescador_Island_(Unsplash)-CC0-PublicDomain-via_Wiki_Commons.jpg&ref=main&repo=did:plc:qzws2ijprqyjicwrzhd2wgcl "'A view from the top of Pescador Island (Unsplash)' by Ken Suarez is made available under the Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication.")

**Vouch Philippines 🇵🇭** is a community initiative to verify accounts in the ATmosphere network and the larger open Social Web. We connect, verify, & vouch for individuals & organizations relevant to the Philippines and the global Filipino community.

This is a free and voluntary project spearheaded by Yohan Yukiya Sese Cuneta (@[youronly.one](https://aturi.to/profile/youronly.one); @[sera.is.youronly.one](https://aturi.to/profile/sera.is.youronly.one)).

Our official ATmosphere account is @[verified.at.youronly.one](https://aturi.to/profile/verified.at.youronly.one).

# Add Vouch Philippines As Your Trusted Verifier

## Witchsky (Web, Android)

1. Go to `Settings` > `Runes`

   ![Witchsky: Go to 'Settings' > 'Runes'](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970013951-awrvezi4vq.webp "Witchsky: Go to 'Settings' > 'Runes'")

1. Select `Badges`

   ![Witchsky: Select 'Bagdes'](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970112502-i2kynr16hhd.webp "Witchsky: Select 'Badges'")

1. Enable Custom Verifiers
1. Click the `Add trusted verifiers` button

   ![Witchsky: Enable Custom Verifiers, then click the 'Add trusted verifiers' button.](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970206141-dfpnn20gk2l.webp "Witchsky: Enable Custom Verifiers, then click the 'Add trusted verifiers' button.")

1. Search and add `verified.at.youronly.one`

   ![Witchsky: Search and add 'verified.at.youronly.one'.](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970368921-tfxv6i4doz.webp "Witchsky: Search and add 'verified.at.youronly.one'.")

## Skywalker (Android)

1. Tap `Moderation`

   ![Skywalker: Tap 'Moderation'](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970773649-t26qwk0inri.webp "Skywalker: Tap 'Moderation'")

1. Tap `Trusted Verifiers`

   ![Skywalker: Tap 'Trusted Verifiers'](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970827762-dyid2of6oi.webp "Skywalker: Tap 'Trusted Verifiers'")

1. Search and add `verified.at.youronly.one`

   ![Search and add 'verified.at.youronly.one'](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970847598-qlexk5quk4c.webp "Search and add 'verified.at.youronly.one'")

1. Done!

   ![Skywalker: Done!](https://kzafkriabiybiwygzkrt.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/editor-images/did:plc:dndcvaqmzkxqoio7zmrvkmqk/1783970868526-5s28tsnft25.webp "Skywalker: Done!")
